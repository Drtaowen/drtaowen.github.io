Research interests

  • Complex network properties exploring by fractal property and entropy.

    • Network Properties: Combined Fuzzy theory and fractal dimension to evaluate the vulnerability of networks, which can describe nodes' relationships more precise; Considered the local structure property and whole structure property through Tsallis entropy to measure the complexity of networks.

    • Node Properties: Proposed some novel dimensions of nodes to identify the key influencers in the network; Modified the classical Kullback-Leibler divergence to consider more properties in network to find the most similar nodes for each node.

    • Community Properties: Consider the internal factors and external factors through entropy to evaluate the vulnerability of each community. More properties like resilience and robustness of community would be continued later.

  • Grope decision making based on game theory and network theory.

    • Colleted the personal opinion of each expert by mutually influence, and their relationship would be considered in complex network to find the most important expert.
