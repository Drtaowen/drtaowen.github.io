About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Faculty of Electronic and Information Engineering, XJTU. I'm working under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohong Guan and Prof. Zhanbo Xu.

Before joining XJTU, I received my B.Eng. degree in Detection, Gudiance and Control Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) under the supervision of Prof. Yong Deng and Prof. Wen Jiang in 2019.
