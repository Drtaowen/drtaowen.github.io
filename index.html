About me

I am a first-year PhD student in Faculty of Electronic and Information Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohong Guan and Prof. Zhanbo Xu.

I received the B.Eng. degree in Detection, Gudiance and Control Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), Xi'an, China in 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Yong Deng and Prof. Wen Jiang.
